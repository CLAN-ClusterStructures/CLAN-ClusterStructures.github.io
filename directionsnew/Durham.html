Travel Information

The Department of Mathematical Sciences at Durham University is connected via various bus routes, but Bus 6 is the quickest (see below for more details). Alternatively, the entire journey is 1.5 miles by foot from the train station.

For more details on travel please see How to get to the University and the Campus Map
