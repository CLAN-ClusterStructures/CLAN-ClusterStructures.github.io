Travel Information

The campus is roughly 3.5 miles away from the train station/city centre. There are several bus services connecting the city centre to uni, and the journey takes roughly 25-30min.

For more details on travel please see How to get to the University and the campus map below.

The conference will be held in the PSC building on the ground floor.
From 11am-1pm talks will take place in PSC Lecture Theatre (A54) room, and in PSC Lab 2 from 1pm-6pm. Refreshments will be served just outside these rooms.
