14:00-15:00 Torsion pairs in the completion of Igusa-Todorov discrete cluster categories.
Sofia Franchini (Lancaster University)
Abstract: Given an infinity-gon having a finite number of two-sided accumulation points, Igusa and Todorov defined
a discrete cluster category of type A-infinity. The completion of this category was defined by Paquette and Yıldırım.
Both categories are triangulated, have cluster-tilting subcategories, and have nice geometric models: their
indecomposable objects can be regarded as arcs, or limits of arcs, of the infinity-gon. These geometric models allow
to classify some important classes of subcategories using arc combinatorics. In this talk we will introduce the
Igusa-Todorov discrete cluster category and the Paquette-Yıldırım completion, and we will discuss the classification
of torsion pairs, t-structures, and co-t-structures in the completion.
